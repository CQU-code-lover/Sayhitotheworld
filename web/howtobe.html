How to be a teacher?

Welcome to apply for a teaching job of our platform : Say Hi To The World.
There are a few TRICKs you need to know about.
⭐You need to be a college student or a recent graduate.
⭐Students aged from 19 to 26 are preferred.
⭐You have access to devices for clear video and good internet connection.

I assume you have confidence in this job, so what's next? For a better knowledge of you, three things (application materials) are required.
1️⃣Fill the blanks -> Click me to download the form.
2️⃣And, introduce yourself through a short video.
  • Vivid content
    • The video will be shown to students on the short video platform. Vivid content, a brief video(< 3 minutes) and a good video quality(> 720P) may be a key to attract students
  • Link is better
    • Since video files are too large to transfer, it's recommanded using links such as youtube, whatsapp and so on. And thus we can access your introduction video through web links (URLs) instead of directly getting a huge video file.
  • Make us known
    • Since you are going to be a part of our platform, at some point in the video(usually the end), you can mention our platform Say Hi To The World and website www.sayhitotheworld.com.
  • We need a transcript
    • Please provide a transcript for the video so that we can add subtitles later.
3️⃣Finally, some pictures.
  • Your photo
    • To leave a good impression on the students, a high resolution photo(> 200 * 200 pixels) of you is needed. And the photo should match your current appearence, or it may cause difficulty when students would try to recognize you.
  • Your diploma picture
    • A picture of your diploma, certification or student ID card is needed,in order to make your profile of education background much more credible.

When three things mentioned above are well prepared and the only thing you need is to send them to our email sayhitotheworld.com@gmail.com.
Also, if you have any questions, please contact us through email. Our door is always open to you.^_^

We are looking forward to your best applications, and have a nice day : )
